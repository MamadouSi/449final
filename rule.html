Rule

Objectives:

Make decisions as a tech CEO, balancing Money, Mental Health, and Worker Lives to shape your company’s future.

Set Up:

Enter your name on the start screen.
Track your three main stats: Money, Mental Health, and Worker Lives.
Face key decisions that impact these stats.

Game Play:

Each stage presents a dilemma with two choices, each affecting your stats.
Visual cues (counters, bars, and worker representation) show changes.
If any stat hits zero, the game ends (e.g., bankruptcy, burnout, or worker revolt).
A balanced approach leads to a "sustainable success" ending.
